About

My name is Gaia Di Gregorio and my pursuit is to merge the gap between the visual side of a project and the logical elements that make it work: in simpler words, started as a graphic designer, I’m moving up to front-end development, working my way through it. I'm currently working for Yieldify, a sensational - fast growing technology e-commerce company as digital designer. I was born in Italy, I grew up professionally in London and Paris. Along with Illustrator, .css , fashion and the web, I still use travel as the most enriching creative tool. Nice to meet you.

VGDLQ

BRANDING, FASHION, LUXURY

VGDLQ is a "faux" brand ables to expose the hidden messages of the outcomes of the luxury industry, using the very luxury goods and products. A luxury with intentional provocative nature: Based on its concealed message. Ideally it should be hidden, but on the consumer side, it is a statementThat he/she is showing off and divulging, Deliberately or not.

ANATOME Milano - Seymour Chwast & the Push Pin Graphic

BRANDING, EDITORIAL, ART, TYPOGRAPHY

Chwast Editorial artwork for the 'Seymour Chwast' exhibition at the Anatome Milano gallery, fictitious milanese branch of the Galerie Anatome, showcasing contemporary graphic design work in Paris. Plastic Trips

Plastic Trips

EDITORIAL, TYPOGRAPHY

Booklet created as starting point of my major project about artificiality. From what is man-made and fake, I lead my research in order to explore peculiar cases and object which are a copy of something that already exists in nature or in history. This catalogue purposes you a range of luxury and artificial places, from Las Vegas hotel to Dubai's fake islands, from the World theme-park in Beijing and to an artificial tropical island in the middle of Germany.
